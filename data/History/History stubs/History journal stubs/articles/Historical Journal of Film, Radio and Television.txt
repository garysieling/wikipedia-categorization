The Historical Journal of Film, Radio and Television  (ISSNÂ 0143-9685; E-ISSN 1465-3451) is an academic journal dedicated to the study of media history. It is published quarterly by Routledge on behalf of the International Association for Media and History. The current editor-in-chief is James Chapman. This interdisciplinary journal considers mass media from the perspective of historians and social scientists. It covers the impact of mass communications on 20th century history.
