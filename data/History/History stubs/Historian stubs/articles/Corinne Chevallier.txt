Corinne Chevallier (born 25 June 1935) is an Algerian historian and novelist of pied noir descent.  Her father, Jacques Chevallier, was mayor of Algiers. She was born in Algiers in 1935, where she has lived ever since. She is one of the few pieds noirs who took Algerian citizenship and remained in the new state. Her works include the novels  La Petite Fille du Tassili (Algiers: Éditions Casbah, 2001) and  La Nuit du corsaire (Algiers: Éditions Casbah, 2005)
