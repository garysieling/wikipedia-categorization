Chip Ghigna (born October 24, 1988) is an American artist living in Birmingham, Alabama. He received his B.A. in Studio Art from Auburn University in 2012. His paintings appear in galleries and collections in the U.S. and France. A portfolio of his paintings are displayed on his website at ChipGhigna.com An article about the artist and his mural "Trees of Ascension" was published in AL.com and the Birmingham News in 2016.
