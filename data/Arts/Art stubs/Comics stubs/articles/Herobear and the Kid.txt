Herobear and the Kid is a monthly comic book.  It won the Eisner Award for "Best Title for Younger Readers/Best Comics Publication for a Younger Audience" in both 2002 and 2003. Volume One consisted of six issues which were then collected in one softcover volume as well as a limited edition hardcover volume. A second series, Herobear and the Kid: Saving Time, was published in 2014 to positive reviews.
