The Bunker is a comic book written by Joshua Hale Fialkov with artwork by Joe Infurnari. A pilot television episode was announced to have been written for Lionsgate TV.  The world population is dying, and those responsible send a bunker back in time to warn their younger selves, in order to prevent it. It was highly recommended by Ain't It Cool News.  Newsarama gave it a 9 out of 10.  Bleeding Cool called it “A beautiful comic.”  Comic Vine gave it 5 stars.  Kotaku called it "a great, tense read with a killer twist ending that will have you aching for more."
