 The Old Buildings of the Cape is a book by Hans Fransen, subtitled in its latest edition A survey of extant architecture from before c. 1910 in the area of Cape Town–Calvinia–Colesberg–Uitenhage.  It lists extant and lost buildings and structures in the Cape Province of South Africa.   First published in 1965 and since updated, the book is a widely recognised desk reference on the subject.
