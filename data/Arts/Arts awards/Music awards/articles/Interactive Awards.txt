 The Interactive Awards are awarded to artists, bands and music organizations that use the internet in an inventive way to market (their) music. Since 2008 the Interactive Awards are presented on an annual basis during The European Music Conference and Showcase Festival Eurosonic Noorderslag in the Dutch city of  Groningen.  The award aims to encourage European talent and highlight artists and bands that inspire others in an interactive field.  Two awards are presented at the Interactive Awards, namely the Company Award and the Artist Award.  The Artist Award aims to highlight artists or bands with special online initiatives.
