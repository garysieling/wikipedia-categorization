Invalidity Insurance (Agriculture) Convention, 1933 (shelved) is  an International Labour Organization Convention. It was established in 1933: Having decided upon the adoption of certain proposals with regard to compulsory invalidity insurance,...The concepts included in the convention were revised and included in ILO Convention C128, Invalidity, Old-Age and Survivors' Benefits Convention, 1967. Prior to its being shelved, the convention had been ratified by ten states.
