A zero-defects mentality exists when a command-and-control structure does not tolerate mistakes.  This atmosphere is now widely acknowledged to be ineffective in both military and corporate life.  The results of a zero-defects mentality can include careerism, reduced motivation and stifled innovation.  Soldiers or employees will feel neither empowered by their successes nor accountable for their failures.
