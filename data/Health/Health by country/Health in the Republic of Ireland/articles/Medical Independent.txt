Medical Independent is an independent, fortnightly, medical newspaper aimed at doctors in Ireland    It was established in 2010 and aims to investigate and analyse the major issues affecting healthcare in Ireland.   The newspaper has received a number of awards and accolades for its investigative journalism.         Its investigations are widely picked by national media and organisations.
