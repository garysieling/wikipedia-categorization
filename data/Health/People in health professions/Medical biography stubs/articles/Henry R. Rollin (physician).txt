Henry R. Rollin, MD, FRCPsych (Hon), FRCP, was a psychiatrist and the Deputy Superintendent of Horton Hospital, Epsom, one of the largest psychiatric hospitals in the UK. Later in life, he declared that he despised the demolition of psychiatric asylums under Enoch Powell, the community did not care and that much had been lost by closing them. He is also credited with bringing music to asylums.
