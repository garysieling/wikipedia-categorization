A crew equipment interface test (CEIT) was a training procedure that helped spaceflight crewmembers gain first-hand knowledge of the flight hardware used during a space shuttle mission.  For International Space Station assembly tasks, NASA conducted CEIT activities in the Space Station Processing Facility at Kennedy Space Center (KSC).
