Simon Henderson (born 1975/76 ) is the present Head Master of Eton College.  He was headmaster of Bradfield College from 2011 to 2015.  Henderson studied history at Brasenose College, Oxford.  Before his appointment as Head Master in 2015, he had previously taught at Eton from 2001 to 2009.  From 2009 to 2011 he held the position of Deputy Head (Academic) at the prestigious Sherborne School.
