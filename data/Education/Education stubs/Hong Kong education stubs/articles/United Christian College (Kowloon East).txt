   United Christian College (Kowloon East) is a private Christian middle school established in 2003. The school is located in New Clear Water Bay Road, Kowloon, Hong Kong. English is used as the language of instruction.  The school also provides church life to students and parents. United Christian Community Church (UCCC) was established by the school chaplain and the school principal in October 2004 for the purpose.  The school building is near New Clear Water Bay Road. The construction of a new extension building project would start from October 2007 to provide a hostel, swimming pool, multi-purpose rooms, lecture theatre, chapel, canteen, etc. to all teachers and students. The dormitory was built for foreign exchange students and also S.1 students to live.
