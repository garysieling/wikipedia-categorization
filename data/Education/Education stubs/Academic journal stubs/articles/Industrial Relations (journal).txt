Industrial Relations: A Journal of Economy and Society is a quarterly peer-reviewed economics journal covering industrial relations and labor economics. It was established in 1961 and is published by John Wiley & Sons on behalf of the UC-Berkeley Institute for Research on Labor and Employment, of which it is the official journal. The editor-in-chief is Chris Riddell (University of Waterloo). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2015 impact factor of 1.59, ranking it 8th out of 26 journals in the category "Industrial Relations & Labor".
