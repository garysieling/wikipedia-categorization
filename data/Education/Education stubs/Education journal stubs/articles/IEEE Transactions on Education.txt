IEEE Transactions on Education is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal published by the IEEE Education Society. It contains articles and other contributions in the area of education research, methods, materials, programs, and technology in electrical engineering, computer engineering, and fields within the scope of interest of the IEEE.  The editor-in-chief is Jeffrey E. Froyd (Texas A&M University). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2013 impact factor of 1.221.
