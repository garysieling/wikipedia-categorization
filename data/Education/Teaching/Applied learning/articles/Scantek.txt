ScanTek is an education program, an ICT-based series of teaching and learning resources developed by the British educational technology company LJ Create.  The program has been specifically designed to enhance the delivery of post-14 courses in engineering and technology. ScanTek modular curriculum is used in middle school or high school. It was first introduced in England and was later imported to the United States. It is similar in idea as Project Lead the Way that it provides a curriculum for the teacher to use. It is a purchased curriculum made by outside source and sold to school districts to supplement or replace their Technology Education program.
