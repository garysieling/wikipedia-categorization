  Education in the Cayman Islands is compulsory to the age of 16 and is free to all Caymanian children.  The literacy rate for residents over age 15 is 98%.  Public schools follow a British-style educational system.  The Cayman Islands Education Department operates 10 primary, one special education, and 3 high schools.
