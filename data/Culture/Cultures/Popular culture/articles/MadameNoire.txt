MadameNoire is an international online magazine that is geared toward the lifestyles of African American women as well as popular culture.  In 2015, MadameNoire had 7,116,000 unique visitors monthly, making it the most trafficked site oriented to African Americans--ahead of The Root, BET.com, and Bossip.com.
