Watkins Family Hour is a bluegrass musical collaborative led by Sara and Sean Watkins. The group began in 2002 as a monthly, informal musical variety show with the Watkins siblings and their friends in the Los Angeles nightclub Largo.  Their eponymous debut album, which consists entirely of covers,  was released on July 24, 2015 on Family Hour Records via Thirty Tigers,  and was produced by Sheldon Gomberg.
