Dedicated Music Award (Vietnamese: Giải thưởng Âm nhạc Cống hiến) is an annual music award presented by Sports and Culture (vi), a prestigious entertaining newspaper in Viet Nam, to recognize the discoveries and creations contributed to the richness and development of Viet Nam pop music life. This award is considered as an "Grammy Award" in Vietnamese music.    The first ceremony was held in 2005, with the original name "Pre-Dedicated Music Award".
