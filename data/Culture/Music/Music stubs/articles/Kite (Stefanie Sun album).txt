Kite (simplified Chinese: 风筝; traditional Chinese: 風箏) is the third studio album by Singaporean singer Stefanie Sun (simplified Chinese: 孙燕姿; traditional Chinese: 孫燕姿), released on 9 July 2001 by Warner Music Taiwan.  The album earned an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Top 10 Best Selling Mandarin Albums of the Year in 2001.
