Dulaiay dashnami sampradaya  is a Dashnami  community of Banghushree, dang in Nepal. They have adopted unique Hindu culture and rituals. They were believed to travel in Banghushree for the business of hospitality where they used to serve people who were returning from India. Basically, Puris   are known as Dulaiya  Dashnami Sanyasi Sampradaya who migrated from Dulaiya to Banghushree [better source needed] more than 200 years ago.  Cultural and ritual acts practiced in Dulaiya Dashnami Sanyasi Sampraday includes Kul Puja, Deba Puja, Sewan Puja, Debi Puja,etc.
