II crnogorskog bataljona bb, Magazin BIT (English: Magazine BIT) is a Montenegrin monthly magazine. It started out in 2006 as the first Montenegrin ICT magazine. In September 2009 it celebrated three years of publishing, thus entering its fourth year as only IT technologies oriented paper magazine in Montenegro, with stable sources of funding and thousands of readers and subscribers.
