Televizioni Teuta (English: Television Teuta, Serbian: Televizija Teuta) is a local broadcasting company based in Ulcinj, Montenegro. The programs of TV Teuta can be seen in the Ulcinj, Bar and Podgorica area, also with Extra TV an internet based platform can be seen in whole Montenegro.
