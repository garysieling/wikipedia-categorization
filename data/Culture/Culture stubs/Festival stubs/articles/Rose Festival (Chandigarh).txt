  The Rose Festival of Chandigarh is a floral festival held every year in February in the Zakir Hussain Rose Garden of sector 16, Chandigarh, India. The 2018 festival was held from 23 February 2018 to 25 February 2018, and featured musical events, helicopter rides, and a dog show.  The 2018 festival also had for the first time a stall to promote transgender awareness.  The garden where the festival is held host about 50,000 plants of 1,600 species of rose.
