Gray Areas was a quarterly magazine published from 1992 to 1995 by publisher Netta Gilboa. It won several awards including "One Of The Top Ten Magazines of 1992" by Library Journal. It discussed subcultures involving drugs (narcotics), phreaking, cyberpunk, pornography, the Grateful Dead and related issues.  It only published 7 issues, but continues on as a website.
