Programa Silvio Santos is a Brazilian variety program presented and created by Silvio Santos and  broadcast by Sistema Brasileiro de Televis√£o (SBT). Airing since June 2, 1963, it is the second oldest television program in Brazil. It is the main attraction of SBT on Sundays. The program airs from 20:00 until midnight.  It is best known as a program of sequential segments, game shows, pranks  and its famous airplanes of money.  In 1993, the program was even awarded the title of "Oldest Program of the Brazilian TV" by Guinness World Records, shortly after losing the title to the Mosaico na TV, broadcast since July 16, 1961.
