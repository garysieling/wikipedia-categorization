Actancy (French: l'actance) is a term in syntax and grammar describing the relationship between a verb predicate and its nominal arguments.  It attempts to study and describe actants or clausal components which identify the nominal participants in a verbal process which may be the subject, direct object, or the indirect object of the verb.
