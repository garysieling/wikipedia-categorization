Volodymyr Lukashev (born May 7, 1936) - Ukrainian opera director, theatrical figure, teacher. People's Artist of Ukraine, winner of international awards, Professor.  In years 1973-1988 - director of Kharkiv opera. In years 1963-1989 professor of Kharkiv National Kotlyarevsky University of Arts Since 1996 - director of National Philharmonic of Ukraine. Since 1999 - professor of Kiev Conservatory.
