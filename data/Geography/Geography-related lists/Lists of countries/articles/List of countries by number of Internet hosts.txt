This is the list of countries by number of Internet hosts, based on 2012 figures from the CIA World Factbook.  Several dependent territories, not fully recognized states, and non-state territories are also listed. The European Union host (.eu) is mostly composed of French, Polish and German hosts. (*) The U.S. figure includes hosts in the .us, .mil, .gov, .edu, .com, .org, and .net domains.
