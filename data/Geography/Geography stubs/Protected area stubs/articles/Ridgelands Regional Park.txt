Coordinates: 37°36′51.92″N 121°52′54.34″W﻿ / ﻿37.6144222°N 121.8817611°W﻿ / 37.6144222; -121.8817611 Ridgelands Regional Park is a future regional park to be developed in Pleasanton, CA by the East Bay Regional Park District.  The concept is expected to be developed over many years; the core has already been put in place as the Pleasanton Ridge Regional Park, a 5,200-acre (21 km2) park west of Pleasanton and north of Sunol, that overlooks Pleasanton and the Livermore Valley from the west.
