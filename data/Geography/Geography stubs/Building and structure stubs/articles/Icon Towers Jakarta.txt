Icon Towers is a multi functional complex of two under construction skyscraper, named as Icon Tower 1 and 2 at Setiabudi, South Jakarta, Indonesia. This is a mixed complex for hotel, residence and office. Tower 1 has 78 floors and 350 meters height, where as Tower 2 has 58 floors and 195.2 meters height. The complex has a podium of 15 floors to connect two tower. The complex is expected to be completed by 2019.
