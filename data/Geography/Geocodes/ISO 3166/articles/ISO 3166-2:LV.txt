ISO 3166-2:LV is the entry for Latvia in ISO 3166-2, part of the ISO 3166 standard published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), which defines codes for the names of the principal subdivisions (e.g., provinces or states) of all countries coded in ISO 3166-1. Currently for Latvia, ISO 3166-2 codes are defined for 110 municipalities and 9 republican cities. The nine republican cities have special status equal to the municipalities. Each code consists of two parts, separated by a hyphen. The first part is LV, the ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 code of Latvia.
