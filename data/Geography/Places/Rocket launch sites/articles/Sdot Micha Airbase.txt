Sdot Micha (also known as Sdot HaElla; in Hebrew: שדות האלה)  is an Israeli Air Force base located near Zekharia. It is believed that the base is a missile launch facility for nuclear-tipped Jericho 2  and probably Jericho 3 missiles.
